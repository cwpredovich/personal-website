Bio

Growing up in California, technology was ever-present and constantly evolving all around me. I discovered early that my passion for helping people and working with technology could expand and allow me to grow into new, exciting and more challenging roles.

I'm still learning and building, and now I'm looking for a new opportunity. I look forward to finding out where this journey takes me, and I am excited to see how my passion for technology will expand and flourish in the future.
